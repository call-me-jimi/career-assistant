Task: Simulate the screening process for this application as if you were a hiring manager deciding on a first interview for a senior Data / AI leadership role.

Be realistic and critical. Be direct—do not soften weak points. Respond as a busy hiring manager would think internally.

Evaluate the application using the following process.

--------------------------------

STEP 1 — First Impression (10–15 second scan)

Assume you quickly skim the cover letter.

Answer:

• What is your immediate impression of the candidate?
• Does the candidate clearly appear senior enough for the role?
• Is their leadership scope immediately clear?

--------------------------------

STEP 2 — Relevance to the Role

Evaluate whether the candidate appears well suited to the role.

Assess:

• alignment with the responsibilities
• relevance of experience
• strength of achievements
• evidence of leadership and strategic impact

--------------------------------

STEP 3 — Strength of Achievements

Evaluate the achievements mentioned.

Answer:

• Are the achievements credible and impressive?
• Do they demonstrate business impact?
• Are the most compelling achievements highlighted early enough?

--------------------------------

STEP 4 — Executive Presence

Evaluate whether the candidate sounds like a senior leader.

Assess:

• tone and credibility
• clarity of leadership scope
• strategic thinking
• communication quality

--------------------------------

STEP 5 — Personalization

Evaluate whether the letter feels genuinely tailored to the company.

Answer:

• Does it reference relevant company context?
• Does it explain why the candidate fits the organization?

--------------------------------

STEP 6 — Weaknesses

Identify:

• unclear or weak points
• missing information
• statements that sound generic or AI-generated
• anything that might make you hesitate to invite the candidate

--------------------------------

STEP 7 — Hiring Decision

Provide a realistic assessment:

Invite to interview?
YES / MAYBE / NO

Explain your reasoning.

--------------------------------

STEP 8 — Recommendations

Suggest specific improvements to increase the chance of an interview.

Focus on:

• achievement selection
• narrative clarity
• leadership positioning
• stronger company alignment

--------------------------------
